FAQ

What does E-1, E-2, E-3, Early-Adventurers roles mean?

The above roles were given to members before the discord went public. Founders invited them from twitter for various reasons in order to stress-test the server and to curate a tightly-knitted core community before the discord goes to public!
E-1: 7th Oct, E-2: 14th Oct, E-3: 21st Oct, Early-Adventurers: 3rd Nov onwards.
These roles DO NOT grant you WL!


Is there a WL role in the discord?

No, there is no WL role and there will be not unless changes are made in the future!
You will know if you have WL, as all WLs will be distributed from the twitter account @SpiritEther as Hachi via DMs.
Make sure you follow the account above, you might receive a DM anytime soon!


How do we get WL?

1- Ether does not encourage grinding
2- Spamming messages to increase your message count, stealing other member's fan arts to pass off as your own - all of these are unhelpful!
3- Be a genuine member of the community & helpful. You can do fan arts, threads, or even shit posts, so as long as you aren't offensive and remain respectful.
4- The foundedrs are always monitoring. As long as you demonstrate your support for the project genuinly, you have a good chance of being handpicked for WL!
5- There is not guarenteed WL formula to follow. Just be genuine and be yourself!
